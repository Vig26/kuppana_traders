About Us

Growing Trust, Trading Quality

Kuppana Traders is a government-recognized Casuarina tree dealer based in Vanur (TK), Villupuram, Tamil Nadu. We specialize in connecting farmers, timber consumers, and industries with high-quality Casuarina trees. Our business model promotes fair trade and sustainable forestry practices.
